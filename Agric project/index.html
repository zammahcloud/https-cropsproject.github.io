Top Rabi Crops: Diseases and Prevention

Comprehensive guide for better crop management

Wheat (Triticum aestivum)

  • Disease: Rust
  • Causal Organism: Puccinia spp.
  • Prevention (Organic Methods):
    • Plant rust-resistant varieties like HD 2967 or PBW 343.
    • Regular crop rotation with non-host crops.
    • Use neem oil spray (2-3 ml per liter of water).
  • Chemical Control: Spray Propiconazole 25 EC (500 ml/ha).

Mustard (Brassica juncea)

  • Disease: White Rust
  • Causal Organism: Albugo candida
  • Prevention (Organic Methods):
    • Ensure proper field drainage to avoid water stagnation.
    • Apply compost tea or Trichoderma-based biofungicides.
    • Rotate with cereals to reduce pathogen load.
  • Chemical Control: Spray Metalaxyl 35% WS (2.5 g/kg seeds).

Gram (Cicer arietinum)

  • Disease: Ascochyta Blight
  • Causal Organism: Ascochyta rabiei
  • Prevention (Organic Methods):
    • Treat seeds with bio-fungicides like Trichoderma viride.
    • Ensure balanced organic fertilizers.
    • Remove infected plant debris post-harvest.
  • Chemical Control: Spray Chlorothalonil 75 WP (1.5 kg/ha).

Barley (Hordeum vulgare)

  • Disease: Loose Smut
  • Causal Organism: Ustilago nuda
  • Prevention (Organic Methods):
    • Use certified smut-free seeds.
    • Hot water seed treatment (52Â°C for 10 minutes).
    • Maintain field sanitation.
  • Chemical Control: Treat seeds with Carboxin + Thiram (2.5 g/kg).

Peas (Pisum sativum)

  • Disease: Powdery Mildew
  • Causal Organism: Erysiphe pisi
  • Prevention (Organic Methods):
    • Grow resistant varieties such as Arkel or Rachna.
    • Spray sulfur dust (4-6 kg/ha).
    • Avoid overhead irrigation.
  • Chemical Control: Apply Hexaconazole 5% EC (500 ml/ha).
